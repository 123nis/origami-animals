Intersting Facts About chameleon

  1. Chameleons are distinguished by their zygodactylous feet; their swaying gait.
  2. Chameleons are adapted for climbing and visual hunting.
  3. They live in warm habitats that range from rainforest to desert conditions.

Intersting Facts About cicada

  2. Cicadas have been featured in literature since the time of Homer's Iliad, and as motifs in art from the Chinese.
  3. Cicadas are eaten by humans in various countries.
